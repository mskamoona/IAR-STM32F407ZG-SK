    This page allows you to control the four LEDs: LED1, LED2, LED3 and LED4 located on the IAR-STM32F407ZG-SK board. To put on/off a LED you have to check/uncheck its corresponding checkbox. Then you have to click on "Send" button to submit the new LEDs state.
